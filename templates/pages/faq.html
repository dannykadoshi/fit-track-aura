Click "Log New Workout" from Dashboard or Workouts menu. Enter workout details (title, date, duration), add exercises with sets/reps/weight or distance/duration for cardio, then save. You can also search for exercises or create your own custom exercises!

Set a target (e.g., 30 workouts, run 100km, or lose 10kg), track your progress with visual progress bars, update your current progress, and mark complete when achieved. You can have multiple active goals and search/filter them easily.

Streaks track consecutive days you've exercised. Current streak shows days in a row up to today. Best streak is your longest ever. Log at least one workout per day to maintain your streak. Building streaks can unlock achievement badges!

The chart displays total workout duration for each day (Mon-Sun), weekly totals, daily average, and your most active day. Updates automatically as you log workouts. View it on your dashboard!

Badges unlock automatically when you reach milestones! Complete your first workout, log 10 or 50 workouts, maintain workout streaks (7 or 30 days), complete goals, or create custom exercises. Check the Achievements page to see all available badges and your progress.

Can't find an exercise in our library? Create your own! Go to Workouts ‚Üí Exercise Library ‚Üí Add Exercise. Your custom exercises appear at the top of the exercise dropdown when logging workouts, marked with a ‚ú® star. Only you can see and use your custom exercises.

Save your favorite workouts as templates for quick logging! View any workout, click "Save as Template", give it a name. Then go to Workouts ‚Üí Workout Templates and click "Use Template" to create a new workout with the same exercises. Perfect for recurring routines!

Go to Workouts ‚Üí Calendar View to see your training schedule in a monthly calendar format. Days with workouts are highlighted, today is marked with a pink badge, and you can see workout counts and durations. Click workout titles to view details. Navigate between months to see your entire history!
